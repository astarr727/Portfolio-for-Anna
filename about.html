About

My story

I have always been an Arkansan searching for fun activities and secret places around the state. Many restraunts can be considered my favorite. However my top choice is Little Ceasars or Taco Bell.

I recieved my Bachelors in Communication with an emphasis in social media from Liberty University online. I studied online due to how hard it is for me to focus in the classroom and this was also a more affordable alternative. My courses where mostly creative courses including Intro. to Graphic Design, Media Writing, Art as Communication and more.

Now having my Bachelor's while finishing up CareerFoundry I plan to work a regular in person job at a hotel or a restraunt to help be able and afford to travel to interview sites outside of my home state. Don't get me wrong Arkansas is a great place to live however I am also wanting to travel and see more places I have never been to before.

With experience in graphic design from being a social media manager volunteer, I have also gained experince with individual practice as well. Since I am just a beginner there are of course tasks I may not know 100% but I know with short instruction it could be a new task that turns into a well understood task.
